Welcome to Nepal’s Lakes

Nepal is home to some of the most stunning and peaceful lakes in the world. Surrounded by snow-capped mountains and lush green forests, these lakes offer breathtaking natural beauty and cultural significance. From the serene waters of Phewa Lake in Pokhara to the sacred Gosaikunda Lake in the Himalayas, each lake tells its own unique story. Whether you're looking to relax by the shore, go boating, or explore the spiritual side of Nepal, the lakes are a perfect escape into nature.
